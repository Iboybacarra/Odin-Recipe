Sizzling Hotdog Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 Tbsp soy Sauce
  • 1 Cup Del Monte Ketchup
  • 1 Tbsp Hot Sauce
  • 2 Tbsp Oil
  • 500g Hotdog
  • Onion
  • Bell pepper
  • Margaring
  • Siling Haba

Procedure

  1. Mix Soy sauce and Del Monte ketchup
  2. Saute Hotdog
  3. Mix Hotdog And the Sauce
